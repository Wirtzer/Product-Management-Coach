# AI Product Strategy — Lesson Plan

**Updated:** 2026-01-01

## Current Status
New learner. No sessions completed yet. Begin with diagnostic to establish baseline.

## What to Cover First
1. **Moat analysis for AI products** — Understand what constitutes a defensible advantage in AI: data flywheels, model quality, distribution, network effects, switching costs. Analyze why some AI startups are defensible and others are "thin wrappers."
2. **Build vs. buy decisions** — Learn the framework for deciding when to build proprietary AI vs. using foundation model APIs. Understand the tradeoffs: cost, latency, customization, vendor lock-in, data privacy.
3. **AI deployment patterns** — Understand common patterns: on-device vs. cloud, batch vs. real-time, human-in-the-loop vs. autonomous. Each has product implications.

## Recommended First Exercise
Pick an AI product you use regularly (e.g., a writing assistant, image generator, or recommendation system). Analyze its moat: What's defensible? What could a competitor replicate in 6 months? What would you invest in to widen the moat? Present your analysis in 3 minutes.

## Phase Progression
- Observe → Analyze (Guided) → Analyze (Solo) → Synthesize → Create
- Current phase: Observe (study expert analyses, learn what "good" looks like)
