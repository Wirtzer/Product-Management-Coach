# Product Sense and Design — Lesson Plan

**Updated:** 2026-01-01

## Current Status
New learner. No sessions completed yet. Begin with diagnostic to establish baseline.

## What to Cover First
1. **User needs analysis** — Learn to systematically identify user pain points and unmet needs. Practice the "who is the user, what is their problem, how do they solve it today" framework.
2. **Product critique** — Study 2-3 well-known product decisions (e.g., a product launch or pivot) and analyze why they succeeded or failed.
3. **Metrics design basics** — Understand how to define success metrics for a product feature, including leading vs. lagging indicators.

## Recommended First Exercise
Choose a product you use daily. Identify its top 3 user needs and evaluate how well it addresses each. Then propose one improvement and define a success metric for it. Present your analysis in 3 minutes.

## Phase Progression
- Observe → Analyze (Guided) → Analyze (Solo) → Synthesize → Create
- Current phase: Observe (study expert analyses, learn what "good" looks like)

## Key Dimensions
- Core concepts, practical application, current research, mathematical intuition
