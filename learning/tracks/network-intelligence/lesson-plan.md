# Network Intelligence — Lesson Plan

**Updated:** 2026-01-01

## Current Status
New learner. No sessions completed yet. Begin with diagnostic to establish baseline.

## What to Cover First
1. **Talent tracking** — Learn to read talent movements as signals. When a VP of Engineering leaves a major company, what does that tell you? When a cluster of researchers moves to the same startup, what does that signal about the startup's trajectory?
2. **Funding signals** — Understand how to interpret funding rounds: what a Series A vs. Series D means for a company's stage, what investor composition signals, and how funding velocity indicates market confidence.
3. **Ecosystem mapping** — Build mental maps of how companies, researchers, investors, and technologies relate to each other. Practice identifying clusters and adjacencies.

## Recommended First Exercise
Pick an AI sub-field you're interested in (e.g., robotics, language models, computer vision). Name 5 companies in that space, their key people (founders/CTOs), and their most recent funding round. The coach will help you build a relationship map and identify patterns you might have missed.
