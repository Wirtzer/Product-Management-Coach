# AI Industry Landscape — Lesson Plan

**Updated:** 2026-01-01

## Current Status
New learner. No sessions completed yet. Begin with diagnostic to establish baseline.

## What to Cover First
1. **Foundation model companies** — Map the major players building frontier models (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google DeepMind, Meta AI, Mistral, etc.). Understand their strategies, funding, and differentiation.
2. **Robotics startups** — Survey the current robotics landscape: who's building humanoid robots, manipulation systems, autonomous vehicles. Understand where AI meets physical hardware.
3. **Infrastructure players** — Understand the picks-and-shovels layer: compute providers (NVIDIA, cloud providers), data infrastructure, MLOps tooling. These companies often have the most defensible positions.

## Recommended First Exercise
Name 10 AI companies from memory, sorted by what they actually do (not what the hype says). For each, state in one sentence: what they build, who pays for it, and what their moat is. The coach will fill gaps and challenge assumptions.

## Phase Progression
- Survey → Deep Dive → Pattern Recognition → Synthesis → Forecasting
- Current phase: Survey (map the landscape: key players, segments, funding patterns)

## Key Dimensions
- Core concepts, practical application, current research, mathematical intuition
