# PM Frameworks and Execution — Lesson Plan

**Updated:** 2026-01-01

## Current Status
New learner. No sessions completed yet. Begin with diagnostic to establish baseline.

## What to Cover First
1. **CIRCLES framework** — The go-to for product design questions. Learn the structure: Comprehend, Identify, Report, Cut, List, Evaluate, Summarize. Practice on a sample product question.
2. **RICE prioritization** — Understand Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort scoring. Apply it to a hypothetical backlog of 5 features.
3. **North Star Metrics** — Learn how to identify the single metric that best captures the value a product delivers. Compare good vs. bad North Star choices.

## Recommended First Exercise
You have a hypothetical product backlog with 5 features. Use RICE to score and prioritize them. Explain your reasoning for each score, especially the Confidence component (the one most PMs get wrong). The coach will challenge your scoring assumptions.

## Phase Progression
- Learn → Apply (Guided) → Apply (Solo) → Transfer → Adapt
- Current phase: Learn (understand structure, purpose, and canonical examples)
