## About This Collection

Lenny Rachitsky's podcast is one of the most influential resources in product management, featuring conversations with top operators from companies like Airbnb, Stripe, Figma, Duolingo, Meta, Slack, and many more. This library organizes the most valuable, actionable insights from 136 episodes into topic-based reference files designed for working PMs and interview preparation.

- **Cross-references:** When multiple guests converge on the same insight, it's noted — these are the highest-signal takeaways
