# Books Directory

This directory is for your own book notes, summaries, and takeaways.

**Important:** Do NOT add copyrighted content here. Instead, write your own summaries and key takeaways from books you've read. The knowledge pipeline will score and route your notes to the relevant learning tracks.

## Recommended Format

Create one `.md` file per book:

```markdown
# Book Title — Author Name

## Key Takeaways
- Takeaway 1
- Takeaway 2

## Concepts Relevant to PM
- Concept with explanation
- Framework or mental model

## Quotes & Ideas
- Notable quote + your reaction
- Idea you want to explore further

## How This Applies to Interviews
- Connection to PM interview topics
```

## Suggested Reading (Add Your Own Notes)

Great books for PM development (write your own summaries after reading):
- Product strategy and vision books
- Design thinking and user research books
- Analytics and experimentation books
- Leadership and influence books
- Technology and AI/ML foundations books

Run `./scripts/feed-knowledge-queue.sh` after adding new files to process them into the learning pipeline.
